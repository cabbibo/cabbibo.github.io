About

Cabbibo is a Simulation Artist based in Los Angeles. Their work focuses on the physicality of our digital environments, the beauty of movement, and the synesthetic properties of code — audiovisual experiences, interactive pieces built from recursive algorithms, real-time procedural music worlds, VR/spatial art, live performance, and open-source graphics tools.

They have exhibited at SFMoMA, sp[a]ce, the Museum of Other Realities, Sundance, and Acute Art, and collaborated with artists including Olafur Eliasson, Chris Milk, SOPHIE and Jónsi. They have completed commissions and residencies for Unity (first VR Artist in Residence), Adobe, Google, and Dynamicland.
